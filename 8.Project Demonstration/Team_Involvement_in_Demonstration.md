# Team Involvement in Demonstration — EduGenie AI Assistant

## Team Members and Demonstration Responsibilities

| S.No | Team Member | Responsibility During Demonstration                                                                              |
| ---- | ----------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1    | Team Leader | Introduce the project, explain objectives, architecture, and coordinate the complete demonstration.              |
| 2    | Member 2    | Demonstrate user registration, Email OTP verification, secure login, and guest login functionality.              |
| 3    | Member 3    | Demonstrate AI-powered learning features including factual Q&A, concept explanation, and text summarization.     |
| 4    | Member 4    | Demonstrate MCQ quiz generation, adaptive learning roadmap, and explain backend API integration.                 |
| 5    | Member 5    | Present the user interface, explain future scope, scalability, challenges faced, and conclude the demonstration. |

## Demonstration Workflow

### Team Leader

- Welcome the evaluators.
- Introduce EduGenie AI Assistant.
- Explain the project motivation.
- Briefly describe the technology stack.
- Hand over the demonstration to the next member.

## Team Collaboration Summary

The EduGenie project was developed collaboratively, with each team member contributing to specific modules while maintaining continuous communication throughout the development process. Regular discussions, progress reviews, and integration testing ensured smooth coordination among all members.

The final demonstration is organized so that every team member actively participates by presenting the module they were responsible for, ensuring equal contribution and a comprehensive explanation of the project.

---

## Expected Outcome

By dividing the demonstration among all team members:

- Every member actively participates.
- Each project module is explained by its contributor.
- The demonstration remains structured and organized.
- Evaluators gain a complete understanding of the project implementation.
- Team collaboration and coordination are effectively showcased.
