Why EduGenie?

EduGenie is a cutting-edge educational assistant built to support students' learning paths. We recognize that sometimes you need the power of state-of-the-art cloud language models, while other times you require speed and resource efficiency.

By leveraging a hybrid architecture, EduGenie executes heavy reasoning tasks on Gemini in the cloud while delegating concept explanations to a lightweight, dedicated model running locally. This makes advanced AI tutoring more accessible, cost-effective, and fast.

Hybrid AI Architecture
  • ☁️

    Cloud AI (Gemini 2.5 Flash)

    Powers real-time Q&A, adaptive quizzes, summarizations, and complex resource roadmap recommendation trees.

  • 💻

    Local AI (LaMini-Flan-T5-783M)

    Executes fast, resource-friendly conceptual explanations locally on your CPU/GPU.
